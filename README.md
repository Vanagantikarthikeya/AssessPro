# 🚀 AssessPro

A robust online exam platform for companies to conduct professional assessments and track candidate performance securely.

AI-Powered Online Examination & Certification Platform

AssessPro is a comprehensive online assessment platform designed for organizations, internship programs, training institutes, and certification bodies.

The platform enables secure, scalable, and automated examinations with AI-powered webcam proctoring, candidate management, real-time monitoring, result analytics, and certification workflows.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Traditional online assessments face challenges such as:

- Cheating and malpractice
- Manual evaluation
- Poor candidate tracking
- Lack of monitoring
- Limited analytics

AssessPro solves these challenges through automated assessments and AI-powered proctoring.
